Marketing of alternative investments

The Marketing Incubator is a consultative platform that focuses on analyzing alternative investment sectors and is based on meeting the investors' desires to diversify and expand the volume of their local, regional and international investments in a way that guarantees access to the correct information from people who are experts in the field of alternative investments.

Alternative investments are a definition that includes many investments and financial assets as well as real (tangible). And it includes all investments except for stocks and bonds. Where stocks and bonds are traditional or basic investments, and otherwise are alternative investments. Of course, alternative investments are constantly evolving and innovative types of investments are being added to them.
