Partnerships between the public, private and third non-profit sectorsz

The partnership between the public and private sectors is an important tool for developing infrastructure, improving the quality of its services, constructing public facilities, and raising rates of economic growth. The importance of this partnership is that the public and private sectors share responsibilities and risks, thereby contributing to enhancing prosperity.
Through this partnership, it is possible to integrate the financial capabilities of the public sector with the administrative capabilities and experiences of the private sector to achieve efficiency and continuity in providing public services such as transportation, water, sanitation and health care.
